# LOFT Platform — Finance Role & Dashboard Prompt (Antigravity)

## Context

We need to introduce a new **Finance** role to the LOFT platform with its own dedicated dashboard. The Finance user will share the same admin dashboard shell but will only see and access pages relevant to their role. No other admin sections should be visible or accessible to them.

Please advise on the cleanest way to implement role-scoped dashboard access within the current admin setup before building.

---

## Role Definition

**Finance** is an internal staff role — not an affiliate, not a manager. They are responsible for tracking platform revenue, monitoring ambassador performance and payouts, and keeping a pulse on daily growth metrics.

They should have **read and export access only** — no ability to edit user data, change subscription plans, or modify any platform settings.

---

## Dashboard Pages

### Page 1 — Ambassador Tracking Sheet

**Purpose:** A full view of all Ambassadors (Affiliates, Supervisors, and Managers) on the platform, their activity, and their commission earnings.

**What it should show:**

- Full list of all ambassadors with their name, role (Affiliate / Supervisor / Manager), assigned Manager, and status (active / inactive)
- Total customers recruited per ambassador
- Total revenue generated by each ambassador's recruits
- Commission earned (calculated based on their tier rate)
- For Supervisors: their override commission (5% of assigned affiliates' earnings) shown as a separate column alongside their personal commission
- Outstanding payouts — commissions earned but not yet paid out
- Paid out to date — total commissions already disbursed
- Filters: by role, by manager, by date range, by status
- Search: by ambassador name
- Export to CSV or Excel

**Summary row at the top:**
- Total ambassadors
- Total commissions earned (all time)
- Total commissions outstanding
- Total commissions paid out

---

### Page 2 — Payment Tracker

**Purpose:** A log of all subscription payments made on the platform across all plans and regions.

**What it should show:**

- Transaction list: date, parent name, child profile name (if applicable), subscription plan, amount, currency, payment gateway (Paystack or Stripe), and payment status (successful / failed / refunded / pending)
- Recurring vs new payment indicator
- Revenue breakdown by subscription plan (Free, Monthly Explorer, Semi-Annual, Yearly Enchanter)
- Revenue breakdown by payment gateway (Paystack vs Stripe)
- Revenue breakdown by region/currency
- Failed payment log with reason where available
- Refund log
- Filters: by date range, by plan, by gateway, by status, by currency
- Search: by parent name or transaction ID
- Export to CSV or Excel

**Summary cards at the top:**
- Total revenue (all time)
- Revenue this month
- Revenue this week
- Total failed transactions (this month)
- Total refunds (this month)

---

### Page 3 — Daily Signup Tracker

**Purpose:** A real-time view of new user registrations and subscription activations by day, helping the finance team monitor growth trends.

**What it should show:**

- Daily signup log: date, parent name, signup source (organic / ambassador referral), referring ambassador name (if applicable), subscription plan selected, and payment status
- Chart view: daily signups over a selected date range (line or bar chart)
- Breakdown: new signups vs reactivations (lapsed users returning)
- Subscription plan distribution per day (how many signed up for each plan)
- Ambassador-attributed signups vs direct signups
- Filters: by date range, by plan, by source (organic vs referred), by ambassador
- Export to CSV or Excel

**Summary cards at the top:**
- Total signups today
- Total signups this week
- Total signups this month
- Percentage ambassador-attributed vs organic (this month)

---

## Access & Permissions

- Finance role can **view and export** all three pages
- Finance role **cannot** edit any data, change user roles, modify subscriptions, or access any other admin section
- All three pages should be accessible from the Finance dashboard sidebar only
- The Finance dashboard should load as the default landing page when a Finance user logs in
