# Cookie Policy

**Last Updated: January 10, 2026**

## What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience and analyze how our platform is used.

## Types of Cookies We Use

### Essential Cookies
Required for the platform to function properly:
- **Session Cookies**: Maintain your login state
- **Authentication Cookies**: Verify your identity
- **Security Cookies**: Detect and prevent fraud

These cookies cannot be disabled as they are necessary for core functionality.

### Functional Cookies
Enhance user experience:
- **Preference Cookies**: Remember your settings and choices
- **Language Cookies**: Store your language preference
- **Theme Cookies**: Remember your display preferences

### Analytics Cookies
Help us understand how users interact with our platform:
- **Usage Analytics**: Track page views and interactions
- **Performance Monitoring**: Identify technical issues
- **A/B Testing**: Compare different features

We use these cookies to improve our service.

### Marketing Cookies
Used for targeted advertising (with your consent):
- **Tracking Cookies**: Monitor your interests across websites
- **Retargeting Cookies**: Show relevant ads based on your activity
- **Social Media Cookies**: Enable social sharing features

## Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set their own cookies:
- **Payment Processors**: For secure transactions
- **Email Services**: For communication tracking
- **Analytics Providers**: For usage insights
- **Social Media**: For sharing and authentication

## Cookie Duration

### Session Cookies
Deleted when you close your browser.

### Persistent Cookies
Remain on your device for a set period:
- **Authentication**: Up to 30 days
- **Preferences**: Up to 1 year
- **Analytics**: Up to 2 years
- **Marketing**: Up to 1 year (with consent)

## Managing Cookies

### Browser Settings
You can control cookies through your browser:
- **Chrome**: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
- **Firefox**: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies
- **Safari**: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies
- **Edge**: Settings > Privacy > Cookies

### Cookie Consent
You can manage your cookie preferences through our cookie consent banner when you first visit the site.

### Opt-Out Tools
For analytics and marketing cookies:
- Google Analytics: [Opt-out browser add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)
- Industry opt-out pages: [NAI](http://www.networkadvertising.org/choices/) or [DAA](http://www.aboutads.info/choices/)

## Impact of Disabling Cookies

Disabling certain cookies may affect functionality:
- Unable to stay logged in
- Loss of personalization features
- Repeated cookie consent requests
- Limited analytics for improvement

## Local Storage and Similar Technologies

In addition to cookies, we may use:
- **Local Storage**: Store larger amounts of data locally
- **Session Storage**: Store temporary session data
- **IndexedDB**: Store structured data for offline access

These technologies serve similar purposes to cookies.

## Updates to Cookie Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Check this page regularly for updates.

## Do Not Track

Some browsers have "Do Not Track" features. Our platform currently does not respond to these signals, but you can control cookies through the methods described above.
