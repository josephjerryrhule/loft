# Acceptable Use Policy

**Last Updated: January 10, 2026**

## Purpose

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") governs your use of Loft and defines prohibited activities that may result in account suspension or termination.

## Prohibited Activities

### 1. Illegal Activities
You may not use the Platform to:
- Violate any local, state, national, or international law
- Engage in fraud, money laundering, or financial crimes
- Sell or distribute illegal goods or services
- Infringe intellectual property rights
- Violate privacy or data protection laws

### 2. Harmful Content
You may not post, transmit, or distribute content that:
- Contains malware, viruses, or harmful code
- Is defamatory, libelous, or slanderous
- Promotes violence or hate speech
- Contains explicit sexual content
- Exploits minors in any way
- Violates the rights of others

### 3. Fraudulent Practices
You may not:
- Make false or misleading income claims
- Create fake accounts or impersonate others
- Manipulate commission structures
- Engage in pyramid scheme activities
- Use deceptive marketing practices
- Make unauthorized charges

### 4. Spam and Unsolicited Communication
You may not:
- Send unsolicited bulk emails (spam)
- Post spam in forums or comments
- Use purchased email lists without consent
- Engage in excessive self-promotion
- Violate CAN-SPAM Act or similar regulations

### 5. System Abuse
You may not:
- Attempt to gain unauthorized access
- Probe, scan, or test system vulnerabilities
- Use automated bots or scrapers
- Overload or disrupt platform infrastructure
- Bypass security measures or rate limits
- Reverse engineer the platform

### 6. Market Manipulation
You may not:
- Artificially inflate sales or metrics
- Create circular referral schemes
- Engage in wash trading
- Manipulate commission calculations
- Coordinate to game the system

### 7. Harassment and Abuse
You may not:
- Harass, threaten, or bully other users
- Engage in hate speech or discrimination
- Stalk or doxx other users
- Share private information without consent
- Create a hostile environment

### 8. Competitive Activities
You may not:
- Recruit users for competing platforms
- Promote competing services on the platform
- Share proprietary information with competitors
- Undermine platform operations

## Affiliate-Specific Rules

### Marketing Compliance
Affiliates must:
- Follow FTC endorsement guidelines
- Disclose affiliate relationships clearly
- Not make unsubstantiated health or income claims
- Use approved marketing materials only
- Not engage in trademark or brand bidding without permission

### Ethical Recruiting
Affiliates must not:
- Pressure or coerce recruitment
- Misrepresent earning potential
- Require purchases as condition of participation
- Create unrealistic expectations

## Reporting Violations

If you encounter violations of this AUP:
1. Report through the platform's reporting feature
2. Email: hello@themewire.co
3. Provide detailed information and evidence

## Enforcement

### Investigation
We investigate reported violations and may:
- Request additional information
- Temporarily suspend accounts pending investigation
- Review account activity and communications

### Consequences
Violations may result in:
- **Warning**: First-time minor violations
- **Temporary Suspension**: Repeated or moderate violations
- **Permanent Termination**: Severe or repeated violations
- **Legal Action**: Criminal violations or significant damages
- **Commission Forfeiture**: For fraudulent activity

### Appeals
If your account is suspended or terminated:
1. You may submit an appeal within 30 days
2. Email: legal@themewire.co
3. Include account details and explanation
4. Decisions on appeals are final

## Third-Party Conduct

You are responsible for:
- Actions taken by others using your account
- Content posted by users you manage
- Compliance of your downline affiliates

## Cooperation with Law Enforcement

We cooperate with law enforcement and may:
- Disclose information pursuant to legal requests
- Preserve evidence of violations
- Assist in investigations of illegal activity

## Platform Rights

We reserve the right to:
- Monitor platform usage for compliance
- Remove content that violates this AUP
- Terminate accounts without prior notice for severe violations
- Update this AUP at any time

## No Circumvention

You may not:
- Create new accounts to evade suspension
- Use technical measures to bypass restrictions
- Coordinate with others to circumvent enforcement

## International Users

Users must comply with:
- Laws in their jurisdiction
- Export control regulations
- International trade restrictions
- Local business licensing requirements

## Responsible Use

We encourage users to:
- Report violations promptly
- Practice ethical business conduct
- Treat others with respect
- Protect customer privacy
- Build sustainable businesses
